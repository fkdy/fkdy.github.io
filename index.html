Biography

Hang Zhang is currently a second year M.Phil. student in Department of Computer Science & Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). He is working closely with Prof. Evangeline F. Y. Young. Before that, he received the B. Eng degree from Sichuan University (SCU) in 2015, where he worked with Prof. Xiaohai He.

He started his research in VLSI CAD/EDA in August 2015. During his M.Phil. study, his research interests mainly lie in machine learning, numerical optimization and combinatorial optimization with their application in VLSI CAD/EDA. Here is the research summary (titled Learning Models for Lithography Process Induced Manufacturing Problems and Beyond ) for his M.Phil. study.

For his Ph.D. study, he is mostly interested in two major directions: 1) Develop algorithms and theories in artificial intelligence and machine learning especially Bayesian programming and deep learning models with their applications in robotics, computer vision, and quantitative analysis for finance; 2) Develop efficient computing systems or architectures, such as FPGAs, highly-tunable ASICs, for large-scale machine learning applications and high-frequency trading systems.

I am looking for self-motivated undergraduate students (any schools) to work on interesting topics/projects related to deep learning, statistical learning, and electronic design automation. If you are interested, please drop me an email with your CV.
